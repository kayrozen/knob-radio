Preset Radio installer

Flash the dual-chip board straight from this page over USB — no toolchain needed. Use a Chromium-based browser (Chrome/Edge) on desktop.

The board has two USB-C ports, one per chip. Flash each chip from its own port: connect the port, click its button, pick the serial device, and let it install. Do the bridge first.

1 · Bridge (ESP32-U4WDH)

The Bluetooth/A2DP half. Connect the bridge USB-C port.

2 · Main (ESP32-S3)

WiFi, codecs, display and UI. Connect the main USB-C port.

After flashing

On first boot the main chip starts a Wi-Fi setup hotspot (named Preset Radio). Join it, and the captive portal opens to configure Wi-Fi, the output mode and your station presets.
